What is Puprime?

Puprime is an online trading broker founded in 2015 in Seychelles, and it offers brokerage services across 120 countries and in 17 different languages. Therefore, it has a promising user base of 200,000+ users, which speaks volumes about its popularity. The broker was launched as Pacific Union but got rebranded in 2019.

This broker operates from 14 different locations worldwide and has a team of 200+ employees that provide round-the-clock customer service and support. It features two terminals that you can use: the MetaTrader 4 (MT4) and MetaTrader 5 (MT5). It has several key features that make trading smooth and efficient. Moreover, you can make use of its user-friendly and fluid Web Trader terminal, which eliminates the need for downloading any software.

Is Puprime Safe or a Scam?

Puprime is known for its highly useful features, low spreads, numerous investment options, and other benefits, but most people also Google the words, “Puprime scam,” especially those who are concerned about putting their money in the wrong hands.

This brokerage is a properly registered and recognized entity that operates in 120+ countries, which goes a long way toward assuring users of its legitimacy. Moreover, the brokerage is regulated by a couple of relevant authorities worldwide, such as

  • Financial Services Authority of Seychelles
  • Cyprus Securities and Exchange Commission
  • Financial Sector Conduct Authority (South Africa)
  • National Futures Association

Since it has a valid broker license and is regulated by four different bodies, we can rely on it to be legit and not a scam.

Trading Fees

The trading fees vary by the different account types it offers. When you have the Standard account, you don’t have to pay a commission on any type of trade you execute. However, the Prime account requires a $3.50 commission per side in any of the instruments. Luckily, there is no inactivity fee, but you will be charged swap rates if you hold any positions overnight.

Spread

With the Premium account, you can enjoy the narrowest spreads, with real-time updated quotes from major market brokers. You can expect spreads as low as 0 pips when you have the Standard account, and the spreads for the Prime account begin from 1.3.

When it comes to Puprime forex, you can expect EUR/GBP and USD/CAD operations at 0.4 pips on the Prime account, and 1.9 on the Standard account.

Moreover, if you indulge in commodities trading, you can expect up to 2 pips on your account. You can find a detailed list of spreads on Puprime’s official website.

Withdrawals

Puprime doesn’t have withdrawal fees for most of the methods you avail of; some have a minimal charge, especially for withdrawals amounting to $100 or lower. However, these charges are levied by third parties.
