Overview of Capital.com

Capital.com is a global company with offices in the United Kingdom, Australia, Cyprus, Poland, and Seychelles. Since its establishment in 2016, it has received multiple regulations from the UK’s Financial Conduct Authority, the Australian Securities and Investments Commission, and the Cyprus Securities and Exchange Commission. To further appeal to the European traders, Capital.com is registered in the UK with registration number 10506220.

This broker platform provides access to thousands of instruments, including real stocks, currency pairs, indices, and commodities. Interestingly, in 2022, Capital.com had over seven million user accounts created and a total trading volume of over $1 trillion on its platform.

Capital.com Review: What You Must Know Before Trading

While it is headquartered in Cyprus, it extends its trading services to traders from over 180 countries, including the United Kingdom. Moreso, this global broker is regulated by several jurisdictions, ensuring the overall protection of clients’ funds.

  2. The Australian Securities and Investment Commissions in Australia.

The broker provides a unique security feature on its platform called Negative Balance Protection. This feature ensures you do not lose any more money than what you deposited into your account. For instance, if you deposited $50, you can only lose $50 if you enter a losing position.

Trading Fees

When comparing the fees offered by forex brokers in the financial market, Capital.com is competitive as it provides zero commission fees on its real stocks. It also offers lower trading fees in its currency pairs and CFDs.

When trading CFDs on this platform, the fees are implemented in the spreads, which are often tighter than the competition. However, suppose you leave a trade (regardless of the instrument traded) open beyond 24 hours. In that case, the broker charges a swap fee (usually called an overnight fee).

Interestingly, Capital.com requires no fees when depositing, withdrawing, and maintaining your trading account on its platform. Also, you are not charged an inactivity fee, which is commonplace among CFD brokers.

Accounts Offered in Capital.com

The account opening experience on this platform is one of the best among forex brokers. It is simple to open, intuitive, and well-designed for traders to open an account and start trading on the go.

The forex broker offers a Demo Account, which allows users to implement their trading strategies and lay their hands on the instruments provided without losing real money.

If you are a beginner, you have the Standard (Retail) Account, which has a minimum deposit of $20 and offers zero commissions on real stocks. If you are an experienced trader, you can select the Standard (Professional) Account, which has low minimum deposits and offers higher leverage of up to 1:50.

Opening an account with this forex broker is relatively straightforward. Here are the steps you can take:

  1. Go to Capital.com’s website and fill out a registration form to apply for an account.
  2. After completion, verify your account by uploading proof of identity and address documents.
  3. The verification takes a day, provided you upload a legal and up-to-date document.
  4. You can make your first initial deposit and begin trading on the platform.

Interestingly, this broker encourages growth on its platform and can upgrade you from a retail account to a professional account, provided you achieve the following:

  1. Hold ten trades in profit in a previous quarter.
  2. Your portfolio is more than 500,000 Euros.
  3. You have at least one year of working experience in the financial sector.

Deposits on Capital.com

Depositing money into your Capital.com account is only achievable if your account is fully verified. The process is free of charge, and it is important to note that the Capital.com minimum deposit is $20.

The forex broker offers the following base currencies: USD, GBP, EUR, AUD, and PLN. There are several options to deposit money into your account. They include

  • Wire (bank) transfer
  • Credit or Debit Card
  • Electronic wallets such as Skrill, PayPal, or Apple Pay (for EU traders)

While global Capital.com traders prefer these deposit options, using cards and electronic wallets is usually instant. However, bank transfers take up to 3 business days to arrive in your trading account before you can begin trading.

Here is a step-by-step guide to making your first deposit.

  1. Login to your trading account on Capital.com.
  2. Navigate to the menu bar and click “Deposit.”
  3. Select your preferred deposit options.
  4. If you are selecting the card option.
    1. Provide the necessary card information like the number, CVC code, and Expiry date.
    2. Type in the amount you want to deposit into your account (min. $20).
    3. Verify your details and click deposit.
  5. If you opt for a bank transfer
    1. You will be provided with the recipient’s bank details.
    2. Log in to your bank account and make a wire transfer.
  6. For electronic wallet options (PayPal, Neteller, Skrill, Google Pay, and Apple Pay)
    1. Input the amount you want to deposit in your account
    2. Log into your E-wallet account and process your payments.

While there are no deposit fees, you will be charged a conversion fee which is often high. To avoid such a situation, you should open a multi-currency at a digital bank. Also, if you are stuck in any step, you can reach out to customer support via the live chat option on the platform.

Capital.com Withdrawals

Withdrawing your earnings from your account can be made via the same process for deposits such as wire transfers, debit/credit cards, and electronic wallets (PayPal, Neteller, Apple Pay, and others). It is vital to note that the minimum money to withdraw is $50.

Regardless of the withdrawal option, the process is free of charge and processed within a day. Also, you can only send money to the account bearing the name you provided on your trading account.
