PrimeXBT Security Standards

Exchange Security

The entire client funds used to invest in trading assets are collected and held within cold storage, meaning they are held in a place not associated with the digital platform and the internet in a secure offline container.

This is an effective security option because being away from any signs of the internet means that the valuable capital is contained within an environment free from the internet, thus, from any attempts to siphon those funds online and breaches.

Nevertheless, the fact that many funds are stored in a disconnected and offline environment does not result in a restriction when processing client payments or withdrawals.

How it works is that the platform also has a highly secure online container set up, which acts as a 'hot wallet.' But it only stores a very limited amount of coins to process any withdrawals or payments.

When transporting funds from the hot wallet to the offline 'cold wallet,' multi-layered encryption standards ensure that an intruder does not hijack the transaction during the transfer.

Additionally, certain protocols and standards have been set up, with an example of this being the requirement of a multi-signature system to authenticate and confirm the transfer. This ensures that no single employee on the inside can breach the cold wallet.

Not only handling transactions and transfers, but PrimeXBT also has a library of different security protocols to mitigate the risk of any possible DDoS attacks on their platform.

Communication Security

Not only the platform but there are certain communication security encryption methods, including the Secure Socket Layer or SSL, which is also implemented on the website.

Just like other online trading platforms, PrimeXBT has this encryption method set up because, as the name suggests, all of the communication on the platform will be encrypted, making it almost impossible for intruders to intercept any communication which takes place here.

Moreover, another plus point for implementing proper communications security standards is that it can help clients determine when they have landed on a possible phishing site rather than the original one.

Phishing sites refer to a malicious method of extracting sensitive information, which includes but is not limited to payment details from the consumer by redirecting them to a website that is made to be the same as the business you are trying to reach but is made by the hacker and is used to extract your passwords and other information through that phishing site.

However, when accessing a site with SSL encryption applied, a padlock will appear next to the site's web address, ensuring that you are not on a malicious site and are indeed on the genuine one.

Password storage is also a huge part of platform security. To prevent password leaks or breaches to your login details, PrimeXBT has applied very secure algorithms specially made to encrypt passwords so that even if an intruder were to have access to the password storage database, the passwords would appear as indecipherable gibberish to them.

PrimeXBT Leverage

As discussed earlier, leverage trading is a great plus for clients because it helps them invest in an asset more than the amount of money they have access to now, allowing the client to make money using 'borrowed capital.'

This is considered a double-edged sword because, on the one hand, the client is earning much more profit than they should originally have according to the capital they have invested. Conversely, it also means that their loss would be increasingly magnified.

There is a catch to this, though, and that is the margin. This is the amount of money they would need to deposit to meet the leveraging requirements, which differ for each asset.

Keeping this in mind, let us look at the competitive leverage rates PrimeXBT offers. This platform offers enticing leveraging rates, with crypto rates increasing to 1:200 and offering up to 1:100 on other altcoins.

Regarding margins, although the rates can vary, PrimeXBT offers margins of 0.1%.
